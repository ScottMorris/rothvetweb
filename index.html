Who are we?
We are a veterinary practice providing services to dairy farms in Southwestern Ontario. Although our primary focus has become dairy cattle, we also provide service to beef and small ruminant (sheep/goat) clients as well. One of our priorities is to provide great value to our clients. We strive to be progressive in the use of new technologies, and are continually updating our skills and knowledge.

Vets On Staff
Kevin Roth, DVM
Kevin started Roth Veterinary Services in 2011 after graduating from the Ontario Veterinary College in Guelph. He has always had a great interest in ruminant animals, having worked on a dairy farm while attending school, and has been raising goats for the past 15 years. Kevin enjoys many aspects of veterinary practice, including delivery of herd health services, emergency/individual cow medicine, and business management.
Follow @kevinrothDVM
Andrew Zwambag, DVM
Andrew grew up on a beef farm in Glencoe that continues to produce pure bred Limousin breeding stock and finished cattle. Andrew has been practicing large animal medicine since graduating in May of 2011 and joined the practice in October 2013. In addition to herd health and emergency medicine Andrew has also specialized in milking routine analysis and equipment testing. When not working, Andrew enjoys spending time with his young family on his small farm outside of St. Marys.
